About

I'm a PMP-certified Project Manager who thrives at the intersection of content, strategy, and execution. My career has spanned Microsoft, Accenture, and AWS — managing multi-stakeholder projects from concept through delivery.

What sets me apart: I bring a deep creative background into every PM role.

What my creative media background gave me that a traditional PM path wouldn't: I learned to run parallel workstreams across teams that don't naturally speak the same language — creative, technical, marketing, executive — all at once, all with hard deadlines.

I learned to coordinate across technical and creative worlds simultaneously, under conditions where delays aren't abstract — they're a crew standing around on a shoot day, or a missed broadcast window, or a global event with 150,000 people watching. Everything is live, everything is connected, and a breakdown anywhere shows up everywhere.

That kind of environment teaches you how to think three steps ahead across every track simultaneously. Good process, I've learned, is an equity tool. When frameworks are clear, when processes are established, when knowledge is shared openly — it removes barriers. That's the kind of work I'm here to do.
